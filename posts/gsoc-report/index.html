In the summer of 2021, I was allowed to be a Google Summer of Code Intern at Rizin. GSoC was one of my first experiences working on a real-world project and I learnt and grew a lot. I would like to give my special thanks to not only my mentors Yossizap and Megabeets but also XVilka, Ret2libc, Wargio, Gustavolcr and Thestr4ng3r who were always there to answer my questions and review and give feedback for my PRs.
